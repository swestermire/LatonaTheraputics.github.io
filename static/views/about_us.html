About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to use light-activated chemotherapy to improve patient outcomes and quality of life during treatment.

Our Story

We are a Chicago-based team of scientists, engineers, and business professionals, all with a passion for developing safer and more effective healthcare treatments. Through the Nano Startup Challenge in Cancer, hosted by The Center for Advancing Innovation (CAI) and the National Institutes of Health (NIH), we developed a business plan for commercializing an NIH patent in nanotechnology. We became one of 10 winners of the competition, and have since never looked back. We created Latona Therapeutics to develop this technology with the hope of saving lives.
